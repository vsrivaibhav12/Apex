Apex Precitech, started in 1996, is engaged in the manufacture of top arm load gauges for  ring spinning and speed frames, draw frames , combers and lapformers. These products are well received and have gone on to become the bench mark  standard in top roller load setting and measurement.

Today, we have supplied to more than 3000 spinning mills worldwide and are constantly used by clients in 15 countries. Our gauges are used both by mills and machinery manufacturers alike and we are now recognized as the first name in Top Roller Load Gauges.
